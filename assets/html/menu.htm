ColorCode Board Game

How to Play

Start the Game

Hit Play button and you will see the game page.

The Goal

A combination of four out of six colors is generated and you have to discover it.

Choosing colors

There are six buttons for six colors and four buttons for four spots.
You can fill the active spot by clicking any color button.
You can change active spot by clicking any spot button.
You can change the color of a spot, the same way you've filled it.
To clear the color of a spot, click it two times.
Fill all four spots with unique colors and press Go to see the results.

Go Button

The Go button applies your selected colors to the top of the board below, the scores of them will be shown at the same row.

Scores

For any color thet has been selected correctly, you will get a black or gray score.
A gray score means the position of the color in your selection doesn't match the position of the color in the hidden combination.
A black score means the position of the color is correct too.
Use the scores of your previous selections, if any, to find the hidden combination.

End of Game

Game will end when you guess the hidden combination and get four black scores.
You can end game with Give Up button and the hidden combination will be applied to spot buttons.
You can start a new game any time with New Game button.
Come back here by pressing Stop button.
